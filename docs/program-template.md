# Program App Template V1

## 1. Hero

**Purpose:** What is this program?

**Content:**
- Program name
- Strong image
- One-sentence value proposition
- Learn More button

---

## 2. Why Students Choose This Program

**Purpose:** Why should a prospective student care?

**Content:**
- 3-5 short evidence-based highlights
- No generic marketing fluff
- Focus on what students actually experience

---

## 3. Featured Student Stories

**Purpose:** Who are the students?

**Content:**
- 3 featured stories
- Student journey
- Student accomplishments
- Real outcomes
- Read More links

---

## 4. Student Showcase

**Purpose:** What are students doing?

**Content:**
- 6-12 showcase cards
- Projects
- Research
- Practicums
- Internships
- Presentations
- Performances
- Community engagement
- Other discipline-specific activities

---

## 5. Career Paths

**Purpose:** Where can this lead?

**Content:**
- Common jobs
- Graduate school options
- Professional directions
- Alumni outcomes (when available)

---

## 6. Faculty Mentors

**Purpose:** Who will help students?

**Content:**
- Faculty photo
- Name
- Short specialty description
- No full CV or lengthy biography

---

## 7. Apply / Request Information

**Purpose:** What is the next step?

**Content:**
- Call to action
- Admissions link
- Request information link
- Contact information

---

# Design Principles

- Show evidence rather than make claims.
- Emphasize students and their work.
- Use real stories whenever possible.
- Keep the structure consistent across all programs.
- Allow each discipline to showcase its unique experiences.
- Update content annually using student projects, internships, research, presentations, performances, and other program activities.
