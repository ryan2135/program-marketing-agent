Why Study Computer Science?
Learn the skills you need with a computer science major to shape the future of software development, artificial intelligence, data science and cybersecurity.
Computer science today is no longer just about writing programs in isolation. Modern systems combine software engineering, data, artificial intelligence, networking, and secure computing into increasingly interconnected environments. At Pacific University, students learn how these systems are built by working directly with the tools and technologies used in modern computing.
Students in the program gain experience with Python, C++, Java, Linux systems, databases, version control, and modern web technologies. Courses emphasize hands-on development and project-based learning, allowing students to move beyond small assignments toward building complete systems.

What Can You Do with a Computer Science Degree?
A computer science major opens doors to careers in software development, artificial intelligence, data science and cybersecurity. Pacific graduates build real systems and leave with the experience needed to contribute immediately.
You will find Pacific University graduates working at leading companies like Google, Microsoft, Facebook and Netflix, starting their own companies and developing mobile applications and consulting.
Our graduates go on to:
Build software systems and web applications
Work with data to solve real-world problems
Develop and apply machine learning and AI solutions
Design and secure modern computing systems
Continue into graduate study in computing and related fields

What Courses Will You Take as a Computer Science Major?
Pacific's computer science program prepares students to be successful after graduation in the fast moving field of software development. Students at Pacific learn how to use real-world development tools and processes, ensuring a smooth transition into careers.  With a focus on software design and systems engineering, students learn how to effectively use AI development tools to implement, debug and evaluate the systems they design.
What sets a Pacific computer science degree apart:
Project-based courses focused on building complete systems
Hands-on experience with modern tools, data and computing environments
Close collaboration with faculty in small classes
A capstone experience that demonstrates real-world impact
Capstone Project
In their senior year, computer science students at Pacific undertake a year-long project working in teams to choose, design, build, test and present a software solution of their choice.  This is an opportunity for students to explore in depth an area of interest or new technologies and topics. Students have chosen to apply their computer science knowledge to fields as diverse as chemistry, music and game development. During the capstone projects, teams use industry standard tools and processes for designing and managing their project.

Build Your Career
As a computer science major, you'll prepare for your future by connecting with accomplished alumni through engaging alumni panels. At these alumni panels you'll discover how the skills honed at Pacific transcend academia and empower you for a successful career path beyond graduation.
During your time at Pacific University, you can enhance your computer science major through internships at renowned companies such as Intel, Amazon and Nike, allowing you to combine theory with real-world application and network with industry leaders. Apply to be a software developer and gain hands-on experience collaborating with visionary business leaders and entrepreneurs to bring their ideas to life. Build your resume with on-campus jobs as a computer science tutor or tech support with University Information Services.
